Called Vith'nzor

In ancient China, the misogynistic dragon Vith'nzor terrorized villages led by women. Led by warrior Lianna, a diverse group united to challenge him. Battles raged, but their unity weakened Vith'nzor's power. With a plan of solidarity, they trapped and defeated him, promoting equality and ending his legacy of hatred. The legend became a reminder of overcoming prejudice through unity.
